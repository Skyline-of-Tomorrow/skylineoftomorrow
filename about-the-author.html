About the Author

Matthew Javier Gaughan

Matthew Javier Gaughan is a University of Chicago class of 2021 Environmental Science and Urban Planning major with a minor in Chemistry and a materials science background.

He works at the Pritzker School of Molecular Engineering in a polymer chemistry lab, where he found his interest in the application of polymer materials.

Synthesizing the research he’s done on the application of polymers in industry with his studies on urban planning and architecture, he has set out to document innovations occurring in polymer science and their potential implications in the future of urban infrastructure.

When he’s not working on his studies or his research, Javier enjoys producing electronic music and watching sci fi movies.
