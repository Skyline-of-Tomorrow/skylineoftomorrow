A Martian Skyline

The Skyline of Tomorrow's Urban Design Portfolio

A Martian Skyline is a sub-project of the Skyline of Tomorrow Project. It focuses on answering the first question of the two questions needed to predict the future: what would we hope the future looks like? A Martian Skyline aims to present visual representations of our aspirational future. These visual depictions can range from artistic depictions of sci-fi cities to detailed technical diagrams and blueprints of urban innovations.

Our visuals are broken into three categories: Sci-Fi Citiscapes, City Blueprints, and Futuristic Architecture. Sci-Fi Citiscapes focuses on artistic representations of futuristic cities in any medium, such as sketches, photoshop, or painting. City Blueprints details GIS mapping, statistical modeling, and diagrams for novel technology and urban planning. Futuristic Architecture focuses on 3-D modelling of novel architectural designs. Explore each category to find out more!
